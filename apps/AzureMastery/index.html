EXAM IQ

On-device AI that predicts
your exam score.

Exam IQ is a CoreML-powered intelligence engine running entirely on your device. It uses Item Response Theory and Bayesian modelling to predict your exam score with confidence intervals — then tells you exactly what to study next. No server, no cloud, no data ever leaves your iPhone.

  • ML-predicted exam score with confidence range, updated after every question
  • AI-driven focus recommendations targeting your weakest domains
  • Ebbinghaus forgetting curve detection catches knowledge fading from memory
  • Behavioural coaching insights powered by on-device pattern analysis
